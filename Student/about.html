About Us

Welcome to the Student Placement Management Portal. Our mission is to streamline the placement process by providing an intuitive platform for students, recruiters, and placement officers.

Features:

  • Apply for jobs and internships with ease.
  • Track your application status in real-time.
  • Engage with seniors and recruiters through our discussion board.
  • Access detailed analytics on your academic performance.

For any inquiries or suggestions, feel free to reach out to us at support@placementportal.com.
